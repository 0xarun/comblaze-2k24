Frequently Asked Questions

When and where is COMBLAZE 2K24 taking place?

COMBLAZE 2K24 is scheduled for September 23, 2024, from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM at M.A.M College of Engineering, Main Campus.

Who can participate in COMBLAZE 2K24?

COMBLAZE 2K24 is open to all college students pursuing engineering or related fields.

How can I register for the event?

You can register by scanning the QR code on our website or clicking the "Register Now" button, which will direct you to our online registration form.

Is there a registration fee?

Yes, there is a nominal registration fee. The exact amount will be specified on the registration form.

Can I participate in multiple events?

Yes, you can participate in multiple events as long as there are no time conflicts in the schedule.

Will accommodation be provided?

Limited accommodation options are available on a first-come, first-served basis. Please contact the event coordinators for more information.
